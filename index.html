Game Description

Welcome to LostVille, a delightful town filled with charm, mystery, and mischief! The residents are constantly misplacing their belongings, and it’s up to you to help them out.

Use your sharp eye to search through beautifully illustrated scenes, find hidden items, and restore the town by unlocking new buildings and features. From cozy kitchens to bustling parks, every scene is a new adventure!
